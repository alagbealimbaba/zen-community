About Us

Welcome to Zen Gaming Community! This website is dedicated to providing gamers of all levels with a fun and engaging platform to play and connect with each other. My goal is to create a community where everyone feels welcome and has the opportunity to improve their gaming skills, make new friends, and explore a variety of game genres. Zen Gaming Community was founded in 2023 with the goal of creating a gaming platform that emphasizes community and inclusivity. I believe that gaming is more than just a pastime – it's a way to connect with others, challenge ourselves, and have fun. That's why I have created a platform that allows users to connect with each other, share their high scores, play world-known games and compete in friendly tournaments. I also believe in constantly improving and innovating our platform to provide the best possible experience for our users. Zen Gaming Community is built using HTML and CSS(a front-end framework) and JavaScript. Thank you for being a part of Zen Gaming Community – we couldn't do it without you!
